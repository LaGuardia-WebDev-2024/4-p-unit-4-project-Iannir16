Rocketfeller

Rocketfeller can be found at 47th/50th street on the B, D, F, and M trains.
This outdoor shopping mall conists of many stores and attractions.
During the summer seasons you can find a large sitting area to enjoy while eating
or oven taking a break from shopping while you chat and gossip with friends and family.
During the winter seasons it truely becomes a tourist spot you can't miss,
the sitting area is replaced by a large skating rink and you'd be able to spot the infamous
christmas tree many travel to see and experience.
Thats not all though the main shopping area can be found underground as it connects to
most of the stores above and many other hidden ones below.
take a visit down to rocketfeller and have a look around.
